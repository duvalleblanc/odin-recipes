Curry Goat Recipe

What exactly is Jamaican Curry Goat?

Curry Goat is quite simply goat meat seasoned with a hearty & spicy touch.

The meal is typically seasoned with ginger, garlic, thyme, onion, curry powder, & scotch bonnet pepper.

The meat is slowly cooked until tender and the sauce is thick and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 2½ lb (1.13 kg) goat meat
  • 2½ tbsp (2.5 tbsp) curry powder
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose season
  • ½ tsp (0.5 tsp) salt
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper...seeds removed
  • 6 pimento berries...crushed
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 stalks scallion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3 sprig thyme
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 large potato...peeled & diced
  • cooking oil
  • water
  • 1 lime ot lemon...wash the meat
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
